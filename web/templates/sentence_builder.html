Sentence Builder

Build Skiri Pawnee sentences from Blue Book patterns. Pick a template, fill the slots, and see how it assembles.

How does this work?
  • Noun / Subject / Object fields — Type an English word (like "water" or "man") and pick from the dictionary results that appear.
  • Descriptive verb field — Type an English quality (like "red" or "big") and pick from the results.
  • Verb form field — This needs a Skiri Pawnee verb that is already conjugated (e.g. ra•pahaat for "it is red"). You can find conjugated forms in the Blue Book lessons or the dictionary.
  • Dropdown fields (time words, imperative particles) — Just pick one from the list.
